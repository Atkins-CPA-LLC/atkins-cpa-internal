# Privacy Policy

**Atkins CPA LLC — QBO Cash Flow Tools**

*Last updated: March 25, 2026*

## Overview

Atkins CPA LLC ("we," "our," "the Company") operates QBO Cash Flow Tools ("the Software"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Software.

## Information We Collect

### QuickBooks Online Data

When you connect your QuickBooks Online account, we access the following data through Intuit's official API:

- **Financial reports:** Profit & Loss statements, Balance Sheets, Cash Flow Statements
- **Transaction data:** Revenue and expense transactions for cash flow forecasting
- **Account information:** Chart of accounts structure and account balances
- **Company information:** Company name and basic business details

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- Social Security numbers or tax identification numbers
- Employee personal information or payroll data
- Bank account numbers or credit card numbers
- Tax returns or tax-related documents

## How We Use Your Information

Your QuickBooks Online data is used solely to:

1. Generate automated cash flow forecasts
2. Produce monthly financial analysis reports
3. Provide financial insights and recommendations

## Data Storage and Security

- Financial data is processed and stored on secured servers
- All connections to QuickBooks Online use OAuth 2.0 authentication
- We use encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) for all data transfers
- Access to client data is restricted to authorized personnel only

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell, rent, or share your financial data with any third parties.**

Your data may only be disclosed if:
- Required by law or legal process
- Necessary to protect the rights or safety of the Company or others
- You provide explicit written consent

## Data Retention

- Your financial data is retained only for as long as you are an active user of the Software
- Upon termination of services, your data will be deleted within 30 days
- You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of the data we hold about your business
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your data at any time
- **Revoke access:** Disconnect your QuickBooks Online account at any time through your Intuit account settings

## QuickBooks Online and Intuit

This Software uses the Intuit QuickBooks Online API. Your use of QuickBooks Online is also governed by [Intuit's Privacy Policy](https://www.intuit.com/privacy/statement/). Disconnecting QBO Cash Flow Tools from your QuickBooks account immediately revokes our access to your data.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify active users of any material changes via email.
