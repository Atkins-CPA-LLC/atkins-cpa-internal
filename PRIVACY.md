# Privacy Policy

**Atkins CPA LLC — Internal Tools**

*Last updated: March 25, 2026*

## Overview

Atkins CPA LLC ("we," "our," "the Company") provides financial analysis and advisory tools ("the Software"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Software.

## Information We Collect

### QuickBooks Online Data

When you connect your QuickBooks Online account, we access the following data through Intuit's official API:

- **Financial reports:** Profit & Loss statements, Balance Sheets, Cash Flow Statements
- **Transaction data:** Revenue, expense, and journal entry transactions
- **Account information:** Chart of accounts structure and account balances
- **Customer and vendor data:** Names, balances, and transaction history (for AR/AP analysis)
- **Company information:** Company name and basic business details

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- Social Security numbers or tax identification numbers
- Employee personal information or payroll data
- Bank account login credentials or credit card numbers
- Tax returns or tax-related documents

## How We Use Your Information

Your QuickBooks Online data is used solely to:

1. Generate automated cash flow forecasts and financial reports
2. Produce financial analysis and insights
3. Provide fractional CFO advisory services
4. Build and maintain automated financial workflows on your behalf

We may use AI tools to process your financial data for the purpose of generating analysis and insights. This processing is done solely for your benefit and your data is not used to train AI models.

## Data Storage and Security

- All connections to QuickBooks Online use OAuth 2.0 authentication
- We use encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) for all data transfers
- Access to client data is restricted to authorized personnel only
- Financial data is stored on secured infrastructure with access controls
- We conduct regular reviews of our security practices

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell, rent, or share your financial data with any third parties.**

Your data may only be disclosed if:
- Required by law, regulation, or legal process
- Necessary to protect the rights or safety of the Company, its clients, or others
- You provide explicit written consent

## Data Retention

- Your financial data is retained only for as long as you are an active client
- Upon termination of services, your data will be deleted within 30 days
- You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us
- Aggregated, anonymized data (containing no personally identifiable information) may be retained for internal analytics

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of the data we hold about your business
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your data at any time
- **Revoke access:** Disconnect your QuickBooks Online account at any time through your Intuit account settings, which immediately revokes our API access
- **Portability:** Request your data in a standard, machine-readable format
